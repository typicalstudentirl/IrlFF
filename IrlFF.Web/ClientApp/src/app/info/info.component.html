League of Ireland - Fantasy Football

  • The League of Ireland is the Republic of Ireland's national football league. Here, you are able to create a fantasy team made up of players from all around the country.

  • Select players based on their likelihood to score points for your team.

  • Select your fantasy team during the week for the matches on the following game week. When all scheduled games have been played; points will be attributed.

  • You can view your position in the league and search for friends here!

How players earns points:

All
  • Playing 40 minutes or more +2 points.
Goalkeepers & Defenders
  • Clean sheet +4 points.
  • Score a goal +5 points.
  • Assist a goal +4 points.
Midfielders
  • Clean sheet +2 points.
  • Score a goal +4 points.
  • Assist a goal +3 points.
Forwards
  • Score a goal +3 points.
  • Assist a goal +3 points.
